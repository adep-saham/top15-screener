# Top15 Screener + 7D Momentum Protection

plus 7-Day Momentum Protection screener for safer swing entries.

Upload your exported CSV files and get:
- Smart entry range
- Ladder buy
- Stoploss
- Target price
- RR color-coded Excel
- Top 15 filtered with 7D protection priority
